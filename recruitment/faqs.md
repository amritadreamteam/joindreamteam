# FAQs

**Q : What defines and ideal candidate for the club**\
We are looking for students who can contribute to the club, work on the assigned tasks timely and manage their academics all along. Consistency, grit and drive would be enough to describe our ideal candidate. Though you may not currently have those qualities, you should be willing to develop them from day 1.

**Q : Why should I join the club**\
If you are looking to work long hours, build a good inner battery that helps you perform under strict deadlines, learn core skills, and gain valuable mentorship then you should join us. There's no better place to be at.

**Q : What will I be working on in the club**\
Club work includes working on projects directly under Traboda Cyberlabs, and also various external projects. Apart from that, regular tasks will be assigned to you via a mentor. You will also be attending hackathons, conferences and other events as a club.

**Q : What are the club timings, and will staybacks be provided?**\
Timing for Boys : 5:30 pm - 10:30 pm\
Timing for Girls : 5:30 pm  - 9:00 pm\
Additionally students will be expected to be present on weekends if the need be and if the club is conducting any event. \
Staybacks are provided for all days and both boys and girls.
